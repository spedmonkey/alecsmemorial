Alec’s dates and memories

In Loving Memory of Alec Russell
.

Alec’s dates and memories

1951

13 th October went into Sunshine Home

1952

13 th October left Sunshine Home with Dad and Aunty Cath McCarthy October Flew to Nyngan with Cath McCarthy and drove to Beemery and stayed for one month. Coralie and John Gilmore and Rosemary. Back to Sydney from Bourke on the train. Stayed with Dick Stapleton. I lapped up the cat’s milk. Cath McCarthy and Dick took me down to Tamarama beach.

1953

Moved into 55 Balfour St Rose Bay with Miss McCarthy

1956

Moved from Sydney to 57 MacDonald Rd Margate

1957

Moved to Sorrento St Woody Point with Cath McCarthy. Flew to Roma with Mum and Dad and met Dick and Marge Condon. I flew straight and level, and Dad landed the plane.

1958

Jock and Malu employed at Jimbour

1964

Nov/Dec Cath McCarthy had a stroke Dec Returned to Jimbour

1965

Michael Dibbs Manager at Jimbour

1966

Jackaroo with Jock

1967

Worked on tractors with Manager Roy Sherman Started training with Neville Bienke, then Herb Rainer at Les Keys Aviation in Dalby November. Jock and Malu left Jimbour Don Graham at Newtown looking after the feedlot 1970 Got full pilot’s licence. 1974 Mum Dad and I flew to Witchita Kansas to pick up the Beechcraft Baron. Johnnie Smith and the bike. 1975 June 1 Started at the Darling Downs Soaring Club, McCafferies Field 1976 Lindsay Richards and I flew in K7RI double seater glider. I was the pilot. I started with Barry Hempell Dick Condon managed RPC. 1979 Gliding. Did Silver C in ASW15 Gulf Yankee Uniform. I had to do over 5 hours in the one go. 1980 Cath McCarthy at Knossa Hospital 1992 Got instrument rating Sept went to Poland to collect the Antinov 1997 Joined Dalby Anglican Men’s Group August.
